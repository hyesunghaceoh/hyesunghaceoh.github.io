About Me

I am a health economist currently serving as an AHRQ T32 Postdoctoral Fellow at Brown University.
My research examines the interaction between healthcare markets and firm behavior and its impact on patients.
With an MPH and MBA from Johns Hopkins, I bring a multidisciplinary approach to understanding these dynamics.
Outside of academia, I enjoy triathlons, perfecting my Korean barbeque dishes, and traveling near and far.
